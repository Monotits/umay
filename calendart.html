About CalendART

CalendART is a new generation AI-supported mood tracker and bullet journal. Examine photos in different compositions, colors, concepts, and objects, then match one with your day. Your subconscious choices are analyzed with artificial intelligence to reveal insights about your mood and patterns.

  • Mood Analysis: AI analyzes your chosen photo to determine your mood for the day.
  • Popularity: Photos are analyzed against other users' preferences to determine how popular your choices are.
  • Color Analysis: Colors, objects, and details you seek in photographs are analyzed and presented as graphics.
  • Bullet Journal: Create an annual bullet journal with colors from your chosen photos to see your favorite colors over time.
  • Sharing: Share the results of your daily choices on social media with a single click.
  • Stats: Analyze graphical outputs in 4 different time periods: weekly, monthly, yearly, and all-time average.
