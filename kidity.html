Kidity

Road Trip & Home Play for Kids (4-9)

About Kidity

Kidity contains more than 2000 events, questions, activities, and challenges across 6 main categories. Designed to accompany you during free time, travels, and dinner conversations with your children aged 4 to 9.

  • Travel: Organize competitions with your children by searching for answers to trivia questions on long journeys.
  • Dinner Conversations: Develop your children's imaginations with carefully selected questions and build a closer bond.
  • Outdoor Play: Games and activities they can play with their friends are right at your fingertips.
  • Jokes & Humor: Telling jokes as a family can give their funny bones a workout and develop their sense of humor.
  • Home Activities: Numerous activities, challenges, and competitions to spend quality time at home.
  • Learning: Help children move forward step by step as they seek answers and link their experiences to find solutions.
