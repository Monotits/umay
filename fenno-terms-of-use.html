1. Overview of Fenno

Fenno is a privacy-first, on-device self-intelligence platform for iOS. The App records and analyzes your real-life conversations — meetings, calls, personal reflections, and social interactions — to surface patterns in how you communicate, relate, and think. All audio processing, transcription, speaker identification, and insight generation happen entirely on your device. No audio data is transmitted to external servers.

Fenno, the App's interactive fennec fox mascot, serves as a visual companion that provides animated feedback during recording and analysis sessions.

Available to all users at no cost, the free tier includes up to 2 recordings per day (maximum 10 minutes each), core voice metrics (speaking rate, filler words, energy, tension), and a 7-day analysis history.

Fenno offers a single premium subscription, Fenno Pro, available as a monthly or annual auto-renewable subscription. A Lifetime Purchase option is also offered, providing permanent access to Pro features via a one-time payment.

  • Goal tracking, proactive nudges and weekly reports

6. On-Device Processing & Privacy

Core Principle: All audio recording, transcription, speaker identification, voice analysis, and AI insight generation are performed entirely on your device. Fenno does not transmit, upload, or store your audio data on any external server.

6.1 Technology Stack

Fenno uses Apple's on-device frameworks and its own on-device signal processing: the Speech framework for on-device transcription, Accelerate-based audio analysis for voice metrics and speaker identification, and the NaturalLanguage framework for text processing and insight generation. None of these processes require an internet connection for their core functions.

6.2 Data Storage

All recordings, transcripts, analysis results, and personal insights are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (NSFileProtectionComplete). Your data is protected by your device's passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID.
