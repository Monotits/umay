Arithmio

Mathematical Hide & Seek Game

About Arithmio

Arithmio is a search-and-find game prepared for preschool children. With 8 different themes and 3 difficulty levels, children develop attention, concept recognition, and arithmetic skills while having fun finding hidden items.

  • Space: Find hidden planets, spacecraft, satellites, and astronauts.
  • Sea: Discover hidden fish, crabs, sea horses, and corals.
  • Vehicles: Spot hidden motorcycles, bicycles, cars, ambulances, and helicopters.
  • Fruits: Locate hidden apples, pears, lemons, avocados, and bananas.
  • Shapes: Find hidden circles, rectangles, triangles, and cubes.
  • Forest: Discover hidden monkeys, lions, elephants, frogs, and giraffes.
  • Farm: Spot hidden chickens, pigs, sheep, and cows.
  • Expressions: Find hidden laughing, sulking, crying, and angry faces.
