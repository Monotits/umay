Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 23, 2026

The short version: AgeLab has no accounts, no servers, and no analytics. Every measurement, score, and plan lives only on your iPhone. We cannot see your data, because it is never sent to us — or to anyone else.

1. Who we are

AgeLab is an iOS application developed and published by Umay ("we", "us"). For any privacy question, contact us at experlercom@gmail.com.

2. What the app processes — and where

AgeLab measures your functional age using tests you perform with your iPhone. To do that, it processes the following data entirely on your device:

  • Test results and scores — your calendar age, test measurements, composite functional age, domain scores, and protocol progress. Stored in the app's local database on your iPhone.
  • Motion sensor data — during tests, the accelerometer and gyroscope are read in real time to count repetitions and detect loss of balance. This data is processed in memory as it happens and is not transmitted anywhere.
  • Health data (optional, read-only) — if you choose to connect Apple Health, AgeLab may read walking speed, walking steadiness, step count, VO2max, heart-rate variability, and resting heart rate to enrich your scores. AgeLab never writes to Health, and Health data never leaves your device. We do not use Health data for advertising, marketing, or any purpose other than computing your scores, consistent with Apple's HealthKit guidelines.

3. What we do not do

  • No account, registration, or sign-in of any kind.
  • No servers: the app makes no connection to any backend operated by us.
  • No analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, no trackers, no fingerprinting.
  • No sale, sharing, or transfer of personal data to third parties — we never possess it in the first place.

4. Purchases

Subscriptions and the lifetime purchase are processed by Apple through your Apple ID. We do not receive your name, payment details, or billing address. Apple's handling of that data is described in Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Notifications

If you allow notifications, AgeLab schedules local reminders on your device (for example, your 90-day retest). No push-notification service or server is involved.

6. Data retention and deletion

Your data stays on your iPhone until you delete it. You can erase every test result and protocol at any time via Settings → Delete all data inside the app. Deleting the app from your device also removes all of its data. Because we hold no copy, there is nothing further for us to delete.

7. Children

AgeLab is designed for adults and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly process children's data — and, as described above, we do not collect anyone's data on our systems.

8. Your rights

Privacy laws such as the GDPR and Türkiye's KVKK grant rights of access, correction, and erasure. Because all AgeLab data resides solely on your device and under your control, you can exercise these rights directly in the app. If you believe we hold any personal data about you (for example, from an email you sent us), contact us and we will resolve it.

9. Changes to this policy

If we ever change how AgeLab handles data — for example, by adding an online feature — we will update this policy, change the effective date above, and make the change clear inside the app before it applies.
