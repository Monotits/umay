Privacy Policy

Umay.dev Content Engine — Pinterest Integration
Last updated: March 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Umay.dev Content Engine ("the App") is a content management tool developed by Umay.dev that integrates with the Pinterest API to help users publish and manage pins on their Pinterest accounts. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Pinterest integration.

2. Information We Collect

When you connect your Pinterest account to the App, we collect and store the following information:

  • Pinterest OAuth tokens (access token and refresh token) to authenticate API requests on your behalf
  • Pinterest user ID for account identification
  • Board information (board names and IDs) to allow you to select where pins are published

We do not collect your Pinterest password. Authentication is handled entirely through Pinterest's secure OAuth 2.0 flow.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your Pinterest data is used solely to:

  • Create and publish pins to your selected boards
  • Retrieve your board list for pin placement
  • Manage your content publishing schedule

We do not sell, share, or transfer your Pinterest data to any third parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your Pinterest OAuth tokens are stored locally on your own device within the App's secure configuration. We do not transmit or store your credentials on any external servers. All API communication with Pinterest uses HTTPS encryption.

5. Data Retention

Your Pinterest credentials are retained only as long as you maintain an active connection between the App and your Pinterest account. You can disconnect your Pinterest account at any time by removing the stored tokens from the App's configuration, which will immediately revoke the App's access.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Disconnect your Pinterest account from the App at any time
  • Request deletion of any data associated with your account
  • Revoke the App's access through your Pinterest security settings

7. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the Pinterest API, which is governed by Pinterest's Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review Pinterest's policies for information about how they handle your data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
