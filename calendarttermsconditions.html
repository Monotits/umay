ShadowART is a personal mood journaling, emotional self-reflection, and psychological self-exploration application for iOS. The App provides:

  • Visual Mood Selection: Choose visual representations that reflect your current emotional state.
  • Journaling & Reflection: Write free-text journal entries for personal reflection and emotional processing.

  • Data Export & Control: Export or permanently delete your data at any time.
