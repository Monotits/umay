Privacy Policy

SuppLab — Supplement Tracker

App: SuppLab - Supplement Tracker (dev.umay.supplab)
Developer: Umay — https://umay.dev
Effective Date: July 19, 2026
Privacy at a glance: SuppLab does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Everything you enter stays on your device. There are no accounts, no servers, and no third-party analytics.

1. Who We Are

SuppLab is developed by Umay, an independent developer. You can reach us at https://umay.dev.

2. Data We Collect

SuppLab does not collect any personal data. The app operates entirely on your device:

  • No account or registration — you never provide us with your name, email address, or any identifier.
  • No backend server — the supplement database and all analysis logic are bundled inside the app; nothing is sent to external servers because none exist.
  • Health & tracking data — any supplement logs, N-of-1 experiment parameters, or notes you create are stored exclusively in local app storage on your device.

Because we never receive your data, we cannot sell it, share it, or expose it in a breach.

3. Data You Create Locally

Information you enter into SuppLab — such as supplement schedules, dosage logs, experiment parameters, and personal observations — is stored locally using iOS secure storage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device through the app.
  • May be included in an iCloud backup if you have iCloud Backup enabled for this app in your device settings. That backup is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy, not ours.
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app.

4. Third-Party Services

SuppLab does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, crash-reporting services, or social media SDKs. We do not share any data with third parties.

The App is distributed through Apple’s App Store. Apple may collect certain usage data under their own privacy policy, independent of ours. Please review Apple’s Privacy Policy for details.

5. Purchases & Payments

If SuppLab offers in-app purchases or subscriptions, all payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment information. Apple’s handling of payment data is governed by Apple’s Privacy Policy.

6. Children’s Privacy

SuppLab is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. Because the app collects no data at all, no special risk exists; however, if you are a parent or guardian with concerns, please contact us at https://umay.dev.

7. Your Rights

Because SuppLab does not collect or process personal data on our end, there is typically nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on your behalf. Your data lives on your device and is entirely under your control. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing its local storage through your device’s settings.

If you are located in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, California, or another jurisdiction with specific privacy rights, you may contact us with any questions. We will respond even if the only answer is that we hold no data about you.

8. Data Security

All locally stored data benefits from the security mechanisms provided by iOS — encrypted file system and sandboxed app storage. We do not transmit data over a network, so there is no transmission-based risk.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the Effective Date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
